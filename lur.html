METHODOLOGY · IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS
This tool compares unit rates paid by different political advertisers within the same station, daypart, and broadcast week. Under FCC §315, all legally qualified candidates must be charged the Lowest Unit Rate offered to any advertiser for the same class and amount of time. A rate discrepancy between political buyers on the same slot may indicate an LUR violation. However: (1) this analysis uses political file data only — we do not have access to commercial rate cards or makegood rates; (2) different rate classes (preemptible vs. fixed) within the same daypart may legitimately carry different rates; (3) volume discounts and package rates can explain some discrepancies. Flagged slots warrant investigation, not automatic enforcement action. Est. overcharge = (candidate rate − slot minimum) × units purchased.
