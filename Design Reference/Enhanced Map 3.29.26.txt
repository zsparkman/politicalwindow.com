Performance approach

For phase 1:

load states immediately
lazy-load districts only for the selected state
cache district data client-side after first load
simplify geometries enough for web rendering
use separate hover/selected feature state instead of reloading layers

That keeps the experience fast without needing a full tile pipeline on day one.

What not to do

I would avoid:

loading all congressional districts for the entire US up front
rendering giant raw GeoJSON blobs without simplification
mixing too many labels into the national view
using a generic consumer map aesthetic that clashes with your current dark product UI
